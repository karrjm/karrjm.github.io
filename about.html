About

I am a creative technologist and artist exploring the intersection of physical computing, game design, and interactive art. My practice investigates how technology can create more playful and meaningful connections between humans and machines.

Through experimental games, interactive installations, and physical computing projects, I develop systems that challenge conventional human-computer interaction. I believe in creating experiences that are both technically sophisticated and emotionally resonant.
